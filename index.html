  • jQueryTO

    March 15 - 16, 2014
    Toronto, ON

    jQueryTO(2014) features a 2 day schedule with a series of international and local speakers providing insights into the future of development, design, user experience and performance using JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

    FITC is the intersection between Creative Technologists, Designers, Developers and all-around awesome thinkers from various innovative industries across the globe. They converge every year in Toronto to connect, challenge, inspire and to experience the cutting edge in design and technology.

What is WebConf.ca?

Webconf.ca is a directory to help Canadians find web related conferences in Canada and perhaps inspire the creation of new ones.
