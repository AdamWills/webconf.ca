    Ottawa, ON

    WordCamps are casual, locally-organized conferences that focus on everything related to WordPress. Each WordCamp is a little different, but they all share a similar goal.   • MoSo

    June 11 - 14, 2014
    Saskatoon, SK

    Twitter Website

What is WebConf.ca?

Webconf.ca is a directory to help Canadians find web related conferences in Canada and perhaps inspire the creation of new ones.
