  • FITC Toronto 2014

    April 27 - 29, 2014
    Toronto, ON

    FITC is the intersection between Creative Technologists, Designers, Developers and all-around awesome thinkers from various innovative industries across the globe. They converge every year in Toronto to connect, challenge, inspire and to experience the cutting edge in design and technology.

    Twitter Website

  • DrupalCamp Toronto

    May 02 - 03, 2014
    Toronto, ON

    DrupalCamp Toronto is an annual conference, organized by the non-profit Toronto Drupal Users Group, focused on discussions and training about the Drupal content management system. The conference is hosted by the community, for the community.

    Twitter Website

  • WordCamp Ottawa

    May 3 - 4, 2014
    Ottawa, ON

    WordCamps are casual, locally-organized conferences that focus on everything related to WordPress. Each WordCamp is a little different, but they all share a similar goal. That is to provide an opportunity for people to come together and discuss their common interest in WordPress, learn from each other, network, and have a great time doing it.

    Twitter Website

  • PGCon - PostgreSQL Conference for Users and Developers

    May 20 - 24, 2014
    Ottawa, ON

    PGCon is an annual conference for users and developers of PostgreSQL, a leading relational database, which just happens to be open source. PGCon is the place to meet, discuss, build relationships, learn valuable insights, and generally chat about the work you are doing with PostgreSQL. If you want to learn why so many people are moving to PostgreSQL, PGCon will be the place to find out why. Whether you are a casual user or you've been working with PostgreSQL for years, PGCon will have something for you.

    Twitter Website

  • a11yQC

    October 17, 2014
    Québec, PQ

    Soyez des nôtres le 17 octobre pour l’édition 2013 d’a11yQC : le seul évènement à Québec consacré entièrement à l’accessibilité du Web. L’évènement, qui se déroulera au Cégep de Sainte-Foy, vous promet:

    • Une opportunité unique pour échanger avec des gens de diverses compétences autour d’un thème commun : comprendre, développer et promouvoir l’accessibilité du Web
    • Plus d’une vingtaine de conférenciers proposant des sujets variés s’adressant à des professionnels de toutes les disciplines du Web
    • Des outils pour mieux intégrer l’accessibilité du Web à ses pratiques, notamment afin de répondre aux exigences du gouvernement du Québec (SGQRI 008)
    Twitter Website

What is WebConf.ca?

Webconf.ca is a directory to help Canadians find web related conferences in Canada and perhaps inspire the creation of new ones.
